About This Site

This site is self-managed and built on a combination of open-source tools, practical experience, and selective automation. The focus is on reliability, control, and continuous refinement.

The underlying environment spans multiple operating systems and platforms, ranging from lightweight distributions to enterprise-grade systems. Over time, this has included various Linux ecosystems, Windows environments, and storage platforms, with an emphasis on understanding system behavior through direct interaction.

This environment functions as an active testbed. Current infrastructure includes:

  • Mixed Linux and virtualization platforms across several hosts
  • Private networking and secure remote access layers
  • Self-hosted communication and application services
  • DNS control, filtering, and service hardening mechanisms

Most services are self-hosted where practical. Automation is applied where it improves consistency, and systems are routinely adjusted based on performance, stability, and evolving requirements.

Capabilities

  • Deployment and maintenance of federated and self-hosted platforms
  • Management of distributed nodes for internal services and infrastructure
  • Development of small utilities to streamline operational workflows
  • System recovery, migration, and performance tuning in live environments
